About

ABOUT ME

Yu-Hsiu is an Interaction, UI/UX and Visual designer from Hsinchu, Taiwan. In 2015, She graduated with a BA in Information Communication from Yuan Ze University, Taiwan. After few years of working experience in user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) field, Yu-Hsiu started her MA in Interaction Design in Domus Academy, Italy and graduated in 2018. 
Yu-Hsiu is skilled in designing, communicating, drawing, photographing and a little bit programming.
Also, Yu-Hsiu is an animal lover, travelholic, foodie and shutterbug!
