Web refresh & App optimization

Project brief

This product enables travelers to search, book accommodations, and manage orders via Web, iOS, and Android platforms.
To accommodate 2B collaborations, the product's foundational architecture requires renovation, allowing for the optimization of long-standing interface and user experience issues.

Main challenges

Versions inconsistent ＆ high maintenance costs

The desktop and mobile versions differ, leading to high maintenance costs. Initially, outdated coding practices were used, with separate development for each version, requiring more development manpower and slowing down the product iteration speed.

Huge backlog but time is limited

There are many features we could optimize, but the investment for each project is constrained. We need to carefully consider the scope of essential features and the length of development time to determine what will be most effective.

Lack componentization & inconsistent styles

Due to the transition in corporate identity systems, both standard colors and component colors on the interface are inconsistent, leading to a lack of uniformity with the app's style.

And we did these...

Responsive web design

Convert the previously web pages into RWD (Responsive Web Design), restructure the web page information to enhance readability, and reduce development manpower.

Interview stockholders & prioritize issues

Based on interviews and competitive analysis, gather detailed functional requirements. Use the Kano model to differentiate between essential, expected, attractive, indifferent, and reverse features. Prioritize development based on this analysis for the PM's reference

Design system & component library

By establishing a design system and integrating a component library, FEs implementation can achieve greater flexibility in reusing web components while reducing development time through unified visuals.

Understand the problem

Data observation

Observing from Google Analytics data, the platform's conversion rate (CVR) is not optimal, with an average of 1.79% for the Web and 4.81% for the App. The bounce rate from the listing page to the product page is approximately 38%, and the bounce rate from the product page to the checkout page is approximately 47%.

Integrated user feedback

Summarize feedback from backend travelers' comments, customer service inputs, and app reviews. Categorize them based on the user journey into pre-ordering, during ordering, and post-ordering stages. Then, break down the issues within each journey to facilitate pinpointing pain points, tally occurrence frequencies, and prioritize problems.

Pain point 1:
Poor search experience

Many users have mentioned in their feedback that the platform's search functionality needs improvement, such as insufficient filtering options, unclear selections, and finding accommodations without available inventory.

Pain point 2:
Unclear item page information

Many users want clearer details about accommodations, including more photos for visual understanding, concerns about credibility due to limited reviews, and uncertainty about available services.

Pain point 3:
High volume of order inquiries

Many users frequently inquire about order-related issues before check-in. For examples: requests to change orders due to schedule changes or booking errors, inquiries about order records, and contacting owners.

User journey and opportunities

Opportunity 1:
Explore experience

Optimize the search experience to help users quickly find suitable accommodations.

Opportunity 2:
Book experience

Enhance the browsing and booking experience on accommodation pages, improving readability so users can swiftly find necessary information and complete their booking smoothly.

Opportunity 3:
Pre-trip experience

Optimize the post-booking and pre-check-in experience, ensuring users can easily access necessary information before their journey begins, thus reducing anxiety.

Adjusted IA

Previously, the link to the 'Room Types' page was at the bottom under 'View Room Types.' Usability testing showed users struggled to find this information when browsing accommodation details. Interviews revealed most users expected to find room types by scrolling down, causing confusion.
As a result, we adjusted the information layout during the redesign to improve user navigation and accommodate responsive web design.

Emphasized key information

Details such as check-in instructions and promotional events play a crucial role in facilitating bookings. During the redesign, we optimized the display and placement of important information, making it easier for users to find by centralizing its presentation.

Emphasized date feature

In our cancellation survey, 'booking wrong dates' was a common issue. To help users avoid this, we strengthened the visibility of the date display and added micro-interactions to attract user attention, aiming to reduce the hassle of rebooking and potential fees.

User feedback led to 3 main improvements in the navigation bar during the redesign:

Design system

Ark design system

During the downtime period of the website redesign, we actively established a design system and regularly updated documentation to facilitate smoother collaboration between designers and developers.
We utilized Zeroheight for documentation (Ark Design System), while engineers used Storybook to build components. The establishment of the design system has facilitated collaboration among designers and streamlined development work for engineers.

Results

1% increase of Avg. CVR

The average CVR across platforms increased by 1% after the redesign, with the App CVR reaching a peak of 9% in a single week. This period also marked a peak in company revenue.

8% increase in positive reviews

Although the number of complaints did not decrease significantly after the redesign, the monthly average of positive reviews increased from 42% to 50%, indicating an increase in the number of people willing to provide feedback.

30% reduction in development costs

After optimizing the web program structure and establishing a design system, the execution of a project only requires 2 FEs, resulting in a 30% reduction in development costs. This also freed up manpower to work on other projects, accelerating product development.

Room for improvement

Keep Improving search features

Although the redesign introduced several enhancements to the search feature, there is still room for improvement, such as displaying search history and optimizing keyword search.

Modify reservation feature

Many users have a need to modify their itineraries. As the platform currently lacks the ability for users to modify their order dates or rooms themselves, considering adding this functionality in the future would make trip planning more convenient for travelers.

Enhance membership features

Currently, the platform's membership features are limited to basic order viewing capabilities. To increase member retention and attract more members, additional membership features such as a points system and coupon system need to be implemented.
